Bridge to the Regional Center

What is the Bridge to the Regional Center?

Bridge to the Regional Center is a Spanish-language outreach program dedicated to helping individuals and families in the Latino community access Regional Center services. The program is funded by the California Department of Developmental Services for the purpose of addressing long-standing disparities in the delivery of service for individuals with intellectual/developmental disabilities in the Latino community.
