D.E.S.I. Volunteer & Internship Opportunities

Are you inspired by what you see at D.E.S.I.? Do you want to be part of a dynamic, creative and innovative team of educators and mentors?

D.E.S.I. has a variety of internship and volunteer opportunities available in the Greater East Los Angeles Area. Every day we help our most vulnerable community members live better lives.

We are blessed to have our community members rise up and lend a hand to D.E.S.I. Below are a few ways to get involved.

Volunteer Opportunities in East & Southeast Los Angeles County

D.E.S.I. values the participation of local volunteers to help address the needs of the communities we serve.

Whether it is help with fundraising efforts or offering a service our clients can benefit from such as Music, Dance, or the Creative Arts, we would love to help you find ways in which you can make a difference. There are also opportunities to participate in group volunteer efforts with our clients and educators, as well as group social activities.

Contact us today to learn more about volunteering at D.E.S.I.! We look forward to having you on board!

Internship Opportunities in East & Southeast Los Angeles County

D.E.S.I. is currently offering a variety of internship opportunities for students and individuals looking to further their careers. We are able to host interns from California State University, University of California and private universities.

Corporate Group Volunteer Opportunities & Social Responsibility

In addition to enlisting the help of individuals, we also encourage corporations to use their talents and resources to help D.E.S.I. Clients. We have a variety of corporate group volunteer opportunities available in the Greater East Los Angeles Area, including East & Southeast Los Angeles County. Contact us today to become part of our family of supporters.
